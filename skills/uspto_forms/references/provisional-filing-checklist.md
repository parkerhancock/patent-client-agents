### II. Specification

No claims, abstract, or formal sections are *required* for a provisional, but best practice is to draft as if it were a nonprovisional — the provisional only establishes priority for subject matter actually disclosed.

### VII. Filing Fees

Provisionals are exempt from the non-DOCX surcharge. No search, examination, or excess claims fees apply.

| Fee | Large | Small | Micro |
|-----|------:|------:|------:|
| Provisional filing fee | $325 | $130 | $65 |
| Application size fee (per 50 sheets over 100) | $450 | $180 | $90 |

*(Effective January 19, 2025)*
